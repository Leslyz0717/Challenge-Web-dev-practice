Welcome meal murders!

The most dangerous room for people who think “preheat” is optional. Every year, thousands of meals die because someone decided oil and high heat should meet unsupervised. If you’ve ever set off a fire alarm making noodles, this place is for you. We’re not here to make food fancy. Instead we’re here to stop you from turning dinner into a crime scene.

How to stop murdering meals

  • Clear the area. If it can burn, melt, or cry later, move it ASAP!.
  • Turn the heat down. High heat is for pros and bad decisions. Start low.
  • Stay in the kitchen. Cooking is not background noise. Don’t abandon the pan!
  • Keep water nearby. For safety, not for panic-throwing across the room!
  • Smell check. If it smells like regret, stop ASAP
  • Have a fire extinguisher. Because calling 911 is not a cooking technique.
